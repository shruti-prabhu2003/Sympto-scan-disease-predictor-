ABOUT US

We, DATA SONIC aim to utilize advanced algorithms and data analytics to provide accurate
predictions on the probability of developing diseases, enabling users to take
proactive steps towards disease prevention and early intervention.

Objectives

To conduct tests for the diseases in the simplest and cheapest way.
To predict any possibility of a disease on the basis of symptoms.
To make a single platform/system to conduct tests for multiple diseases.
To help doctors in analyzing the huge datasets and provide them more insights about patientâ€™s data so that they can make better decisions.
